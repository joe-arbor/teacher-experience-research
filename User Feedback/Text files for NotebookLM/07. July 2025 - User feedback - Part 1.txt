Comment: I have grave concerns regarding the Share function in the Custom Report Writer feature, which allows users to send report data to any email address as an unencrypted attachment.

 As you are aware, email is generally considered an insecure method for transferring personal/sensitive data. Many organisations have adopted alternative, more secure methods for sharing information to comply with data protection laws and to mitigate the risk of data breaches. By enabling the sharing of reports via unencrypted email, the current feature appears to contravene GDPR regulations concerning the protection of data in transit.

 While I understand that the responsibility for training staff on secure data handling practices lies with us as the data controller, I believe that Arbor, as the data processor, also has a critical role in ensuring compliance with data protection regulations. By not taking action to remove or modify this feature, Arbor may inadvertently be placing both itself and its clients at risk of violating these regulations.

 Given that the majority of reports generated through the Custom Report Writer are likely to contain personal or sensitive data, it raises a significant concern as to why the system permits users to share this data via email. I urge you to reconsider the implications of this feature and to explore more secure alternatives for data sharing that align with best practices in data protection.
